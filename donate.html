Where your money will go

Your donation will directly benefit the school. The School Management Committee use the money for specific projects around the school that we support - such as the School Lunch Feeding Programme, malaria testing and treatment and salaries of the School Nurse, Librarian, IT Teacher, Farm Manager and more. Excellent accountability and feedback is done by the school management every term.

Volunteers who have a specific skill that would enhance the education of the girls sometimes need financial help towards funding their trip, and specified donations go towards their expenses. These trips generally happen 3-4 times a year and there's always news to be bought back!

Some equipment and resources are generously donated to the charity such as laptops, books and the projector, and our volunteers carry as much as they are able. However, we always need to fund ongoing expenses such as medicines, printer cartridges, essentials for the orphans (bedding, clothing, pens and exercise books) and mosquito nets.

We also need to raise money for ongoing renovation,solar lighting and eventually solar power ...and the continuing development of Bishop Willigers Secondary School - the only Secondary School in the sub-county.
